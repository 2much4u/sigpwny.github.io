UIUCTF 2017

April 28 - 30

UIUCTF is an online jeopardy-style CTF Competition. Teams of all shapes and sizes are welcome to participate.

Registration will open a few days before the competition. You can contact the organizers via #sigpwny on freenode.



Generously sponsored by

UIUCTF is organized by SIGPwny, the student security club at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
