UIUCTF

SigPwny will host the UIUCTF Competition from April 21st to 23rd in 2017. More information, including details about challenges and prizes, will be released closer to the start date.

Sponsors

Sponsorship helps us pay for hosting and provide prizes to competitors. We currently have three sponsorship tiers available:

  • Bronze: < $250
  • Silver: $250 - $500
  • Gold: $500 - $1000

All sponsors will be featured on the competition page along with a listing of any challenges they may have contributed.

If you are interested in sponsoring UIUCTF, please contact hennenf2 [at] illinois.edu.
